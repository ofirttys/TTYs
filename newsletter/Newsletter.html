New EPIC Treatment Plan Features

We've introduced a new screen within treatment plans to streamline fertility preservation documentation:

  • Automated Reminders: Prompts for Pediatric and Adolescent Gynecology (PAG) referrals
  • Easy Documentation: Record FP discussions and referral decisions
  • Better Tracking: Monitor FP conversations across all teams

This enhancement ensures no patient misses fertility preservation consultation opportunities.

Fertility Preservation Launch Update

Ongoing Tracking Outcomes:

  • Fertility counseling integrated into 8 high-risk treatment protocols
  • 100% referral rate (4/4) for solid tumor patients with ovaries to PAG
  • 2 successful OTC procedures completed (both combined with another procedure - in OR and IGT)
  • 2 additional OTC cases completed pre-HSCT in MRD negative remission

Process Improvements:

  • Policy and Procedure document for fertility preservation in patients with ovaries nearing completion
  • New dedicated email: OTC.MSH@sinaihealth.ca for OTC booking requests
  • Continuous process refinement based on team feedback
