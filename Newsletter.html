Welcome to our monthly newsletter! We're excited to share the remarkable progress and forward momentum achieved through our collective dedication to this vital work. Here are the key highlights from recent months:

Streamlined Referral Process

We've successfully established a comprehensive referral pathway for fertility preservation consultations, making the process more efficient than ever:

  • EPIC Integration: New smartphrase (.SKOVARYFERTDET) added for easy access
  • Dedicated Alert System: ovary.fertilitypreservation@sickkids.ca automatically notifies multiple PAG providers for expedited referrals
  • Complete Care Coordination: Gynecology handles all subsequent steps including bloodwork, MSF referrals, and OR booking

New Enhancement: All treatment protocols defined as "high risk for infertility" in EPIC now include prompts for fertility preservation referrals. A new smartphrase for disclosure discussion documentation is coming soon!

Launch Update: Successfully launched with the Solid Tumor team on April 28, 2025. Your feedback helps us refine the process!
📚

Fresh Clinical Guidelines

Hot off the press! New clinical guidelines from leading organizations are now available on our SharePoint:

  • American Society of Clinical Oncology (ASCO)
  • Society of Obstetricians and Gynecologists of Canada (SOGC)

Key Message: All individuals undergoing gonadotoxic therapy should discuss potential fertility impacts with their healthcare provider and receive expedited referrals to fertility specialists.

Must-Read: Don't miss the powerful patient perspective piece "What I Wish I Had Known: A Pediatric Oncologist's Transition to Survivorship Care" by Dr. Ana Carolina Izurieta-Pacheco.
